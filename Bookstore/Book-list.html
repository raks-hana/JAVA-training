Book List:

Red Queen

Author: Victoria Aveyard

A dystopian fantasy where a low-born girl with rare powers disrupts a world divided by blood color.

Lost in the Never Woods

Author: Aiden Thomas

A dark Peter Pan retelling where Wendy must uncover the truth behind missing children, including her own lost brothers.

Snow Like Ashes

Author: Sara Raasch

A YA fantasy about a warrior fighting to restore her fallen kingdom and discover her true destiny.

Curse of Infiniti

Author: Rachel Hetrick

A magical adventure where a girl’s fate intertwines with ancient secrets and powerful curses.

The Cruel Prince

Author: Holly Black

A dark fae fantasy where a mortal girl schemes her way into the dangerous politics of the fairy world.

The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo

Author: Taylor Jenkins Reid

A historical fiction novel about a Hollywood icon revealing her scandalous past and hidden love story.
